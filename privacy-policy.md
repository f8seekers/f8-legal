F8 Privacy Policy
Effective date: 7 March 2026  ·  Last updated: 7 March 2026
Welcome to F8 (we, our, or us). F8 is a BaZi (Four Pillars of Destiny) self-discovery app that helps you understand your elemental blueprint and personal destiny cycles. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.
By using F8, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

1. Information We Collect
1.1 Information You Provide
When you use F8, you may provide the following information to generate your BaZi chart:
• Your name (display only — used to personalise your reading)
• Your date of birth (day, month, year)
• Your time of birth (optional)
• Your city or location of birth (used to calculate solar time correction)
• Your gender (used to determine the direction of your Luck Pillars)

This information is processed entirely on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. We do not store your birth data.
1.2 Automatically Collected Information
If you create an account or make a purchase, we may collect:
• Email address (for account creation and subscription management)
• Device identifiers and operating system (for crash reporting and analytics)
• Purchase history and subscription status (processed by Google Play or Apple App Store)

We use Supabase for account management and RevenueCat for subscription management. These services may collect certain technical data as described in their own privacy policies.
1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect
• We do not collect your precise GPS location
• We do not access your contacts, camera, microphone, or photos
• We do not collect biometric data
• We do not sell your data to third parties
• We do not use your data for advertising purposes

2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect to:
• Generate and display your personalised BaZi chart and readings
• Manage your account and subscription status
• Provide customer support
• Improve app stability and performance
• Comply with legal obligations

Your birth data (name, date, time, location, gender) is used solely to calculate your chart and is never shared, sold, or transmitted to any third party.

3. Data Storage and Security
Your birth data is stored locally on your device only. It is not uploaded to any server.
If you create an account, your email address and subscription status are stored securely using Supabase, which uses industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit.
We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services
F8 uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

• Google Play (purchases and billing) — policies.google.com/privacy
• Supabase (account management) — supabase.com/privacy
• RevenueCat (subscription management) — revenuecat.com/privacy

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these services.

5. Children's Privacy
F8 is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6. Your Rights
Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:
• Right to access the personal data we hold about you
• Right to correct inaccurate data
• Right to request deletion of your data ('right to be forgotten')
• Right to withdraw consent at any time
• Right to data portability

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address below. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Retention
We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.
Birth data entered into the app is stored only on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app or clear app data.

8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the 'Last updated' date at the top of this page and, where the changes are significant, notify you via the app or email.
Your continued use of F8 after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
